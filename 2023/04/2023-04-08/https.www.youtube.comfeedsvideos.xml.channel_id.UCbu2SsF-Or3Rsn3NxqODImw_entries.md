Get another glimpse at Escape from Tarkov Arena a standalone game project. It's a session based multiplayer FPS with the same hardcore game mechanics of Escape from Tarkov.

You will take part in gladiatorial battles in various arenas of the city of Tarkov, organized by a mysterious group of Arena Masters led by the Host.

Escape from Tarkov Arena will have PvP and PvE game modes, ratings, weapon and gear unlocks and unique features for owners of the main game, such as playing as your main profile character.
