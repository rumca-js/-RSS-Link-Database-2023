In conclusion, Tchia puts a strong first impression with its faithful rendition of a hidden tropical paradise and fun traversal mechanics. But the game quickly tends to devolve into a subpar open world experience complete with all the trappings that come with that description. 

Boring mission design and repetitive side activities can quickly come together to make this experience a dull time, which is a shame considering that Tchia could have been so much more.
