<article>
	<section class="page">
<p>New employees who start a job feeling undertrained and disconnected from their work environment are far more likely to quit than those who have a good onboarding experience.</p><p>With the unemployment rate <a href="https://www.computerworld.com/article/3542681/how-many-jobs-are-available-in-technology.html">lower than it has been in decades</a> — even more so in technology fields — job candidates more often than not field multiple offers.
