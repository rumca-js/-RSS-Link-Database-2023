The Legend of Heroes: Trails to Azure is an excellent new addition to the franchise. Wonderfully closing off the story arc, Trails to Azure only really suffers from being a sequel to Trails from Zero, since you need to know quite a bit about that game before truly understanding the characters and their motivations in Trails to Azure. 

Aside from that and a few minor problems with pacing, however, Trails to Azure is a fantastic RPG, and is an incredibly easy game to recommend, provided you’ve played Trails from Zero, of course.
