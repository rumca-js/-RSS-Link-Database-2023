Details

With this server you can:
  • create a limited YouTube replacement. No algorithm, no distractions
  • you can choose which videos are important to you
  • you can share your playlist with a friend
  • you can create your own 'database' of video links. You decide what is important
  • you do not have to install anything besides this package, no databases, no setup is required
  • just download source and install on your system: github
