## Opening Facebook Links Externally
 - [https://www.jefftk.com/p/opening-facebook-links-externally](https://www.jefftk.com/p/opening-facebook-links-externally)
 - RSS feed: https://www.jefftk.com/news.rss

<p><span>

When I'm on my phone and click a link in Facebook I want it to open in
my normal browser, but it defaults to an "in-app" browser.  I
really don't like in-app browsers, and I think </span>

<a href="https://infrequently.org/2021/07/hobsons-browser/">Alex
Russell's explanation</a> of their problems is very good.  I've turned
this behavior off, but Facebook regularly forgets my decision, most
recently a few weeks ago.  The setting had moved since 

<a href="https://www.jefftk.com/p/selecting-the-default-browser-in-facebook-on-android">the
last time I did this</a>, so here are screenshots showing how to turn
it off on Android:

in the upper right, and scroll to the bottom:
