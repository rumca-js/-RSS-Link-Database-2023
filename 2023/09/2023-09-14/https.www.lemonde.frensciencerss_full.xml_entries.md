## French study reopens the debate on the impact of screen time for kids

 - RSS feed: https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/rss_full.xml
 - date published: 2023-09-14T10:29:10+00:00

An analysis of a large sample of children suggests that the context of use and family factors play a more important role than screen time. Several specialists say this finding should be treated with caution.
