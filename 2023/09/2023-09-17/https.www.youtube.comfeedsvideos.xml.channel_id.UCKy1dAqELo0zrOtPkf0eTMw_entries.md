Dune: Spice Wars is a real-time 4X strategy game developed by Shiro Games. Residing within the bounds of Frank Herbert's Dune universe, players will lead their faction and battle for control and dominance over the harsh desert planet of Arrakis. The full 1.0 launch welcomes a substantial update bringing a size increase, multiplayer, Conquest Mode, House Corrino, new buildings, military, air units, regions, tutorials, quality of life additions, and more. House Ecaz has also been added as a new playable faction with its own unique strengths, leveraging its mastery of art and a special approach to expanding its influence across Arrakis. Dune: Spice Wars is available now for PC.

Romance of the Three Kingdoms 8 Remake is a remake of the original tactical military strategy role-playing simulation game initially released in 2001 and 2003 in Japan and North America respectively. Being developed by Kou Shibusawa, Romance of the Three Kingdoms 8 Remake is fitted with all-new graphical and game system upgrades to help reawaken the title as the newest addition to the legendary franchise. The game also comes with both “All Officers Play” and “All Periods Scenarios” features as well. Romance of the Three Kingdoms 8 Remake is coming to PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Nintendo Switch, and PC in early 2024.
