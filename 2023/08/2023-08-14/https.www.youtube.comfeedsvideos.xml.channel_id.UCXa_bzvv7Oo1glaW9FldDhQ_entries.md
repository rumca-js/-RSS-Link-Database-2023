Following the successful remaster of Quake 1, Nightdive have enhanced Quake 2, improving its visuals, adding quality-of-life features and catering to long-time fans.

It has both mission packs, Quake 2 64 and even Call of the Machine, a new campaign expansion from MachineGames. Above all else, it enhances the identity of Quake 2, from the feel of its combat to the challenge of its enemies, while staying true to it. It polished what was already a great game to near perfection, providing the most feature-complete version of the classic to date and a must-play experience for FPS fans.
