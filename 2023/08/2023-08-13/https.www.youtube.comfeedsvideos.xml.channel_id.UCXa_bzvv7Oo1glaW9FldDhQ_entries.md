Moving Out 2 is a sequel that takes what worked in the original, and spins a broad web of ideas with those foundations - resulting in a game that rarely gets stale. You return to the town of Packmore as an employee of the Smooth Moves company, and you must work out a number of moving jobs in an effort to restore this now dilapidated town to its former glory. 

The level designs just keep on adding new things to the mix, and varied objective types also help in adding a lot more variety to the gameplay. Moving Out 2 also introduces online co-op with cross-platform support, so you can also enjoy moving houses with your buddies. All in all, it’s a fun sequel that fans of the original or anyone who loves co-op games should definitely try out.
