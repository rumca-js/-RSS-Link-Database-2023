Sports management sims don’t get much more authentic than F1 Manager 2023. Those who wonder what the life of a team principal on a winning Formula 1 team is like have to look no further than this management sim. From managing staff, pit crews, car designs and manufacturing, training, to setting the eventual strategy for race day, there’s plenty here for any F1 fan to dig into. 

While the game will only speak to a niche audience who are not only F1 fans, but thrive in the demanding world of organizational management, F1 Manager 2023 delivers the job experience on all fronts. Even where its subpar graphical presentation falters, F1 Manager 2023 still delivers the thrill of watching a big race that players will help cultivate.
