China claims to have the world's largest middle class. Look at this article published in the Chinese media in 2022. The headline reads: "China's middle class has 109 million people, the largest in the world. How much money does it take to be a "middle-class family"? " 
The middle class, in the eyes of the public, is highly educated, with decent jobs and stable incomes; they are free from worries and have high spending power, only nowadays they are under tremendous pressure with China's economy rapidly cooling down.
