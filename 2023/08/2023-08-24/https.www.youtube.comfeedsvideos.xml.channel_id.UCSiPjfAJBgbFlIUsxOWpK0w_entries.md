Here’s the second single from our upcoming French album! It’s called “Tu peux pas savoir” which translates to “You can’t know.” Larry Goldings and I wrote this song together. You know the Feist song “Leisure Suite”? That’s the vibe I was going for in the chorus. But like…French.
