Anyone hungry for some 80s nostalgia? Because Teyon, developer behind Terminator Resistance and Rambo The Video Game, are serving up another plate of it. Here is everything you need to know about RoboCop: Rogue City.

In Rogue City, you play as Old Detroit cop Alex Murphy, who, after being fatally wounded, is rebuilt as a cyborg called RoboCop. The game features an original story set in between the movies RoboCop 2 and 3 with familiar faces to meet and locations to explore. Fans of the films can also look forward to the original RoboCop actor, Peter Weller, reprising his role of the iconic cyborg.

RoboCop: Rogue City is coming to PS5, Xbox Series X|S and PC via Steam and Epic Games Store on November 2, 2023. Gamers will have 2 different editions to choose from.
