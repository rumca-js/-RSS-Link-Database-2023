## The AI in a Box is a Rockchip-powered offline and open-source LLM device
 - [https://linuxgizmos.com/ai-in-a-box-is-a-rockchip-powered-offline-and-open-source-llm-device](https://linuxgizmos.com/ai-in-a-box-is-a-rockchip-powered-offline-and-open-source-llm-device)
 - RSS feed: https://linuxgizmos.com/feed/
 - date published: 2023-10-27T05:58:12+00:00

Last month, CrowdSupply introduced the AI in a Box which is described as a compact, locally hosted AI solution designed to provide responsive interactions in a secure environment. This standalone device requires no internet connection, accounts, setup, or subscriptions. Useful Sensors indicates that this device is built around the RockChip 3588S SoC with 8GB DRAM [&#8230;]
