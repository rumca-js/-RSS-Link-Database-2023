A week after the outbreak of the Israeli-Palestinian war, the Chinese Communist Party CCP leader Xi Jinping met with Russian President Vladimir Putin. It was a high-profile meeting on the sidelines of the Belt and Road Forum in Beijing. Signs of the two countries getting closer have sparked international concern.
Putin and his entourage flew to Beijing on the morning of October 17. It was the first time in 2023 that the Kremlin leader left the confines of the former Soviet Union for an official visit.
