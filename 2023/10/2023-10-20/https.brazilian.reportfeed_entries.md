## Brazilian economic activity dropped more than expected in August
 - [https://brazilian.report/liveblog/politics-insider/2023/10/20/economic-activity-dropped-more/](https://brazilian.report/liveblog/politics-insider/2023/10/20/economic-activity-dropped-more/)
 - RSS feed: https://brazilian.report/feed/
 - date published: 2023-10-20T15:56:52+00:00

<p>The Central Bank’s economic activity index (IBC-Br), a thermometer of growth pace in Brazil, fell by 0.77 percent in August, a drop slightly worse than markets expected. Median forecasts hovered between drops of 0.6 to 0.65 percent. Also, July’s result was revised from 0.44 to 0.42 percent, bringing the 12-month growth rate to 2.82 percent [&#8230;]</p>
