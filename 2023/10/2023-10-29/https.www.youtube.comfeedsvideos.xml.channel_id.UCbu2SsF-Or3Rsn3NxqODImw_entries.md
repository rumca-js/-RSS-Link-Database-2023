Nintendo isn't known for creating the spookiest games - and yet, there are some truly terrifying moments in Mario, Zelda, and even Pokemon.

From easter eggs in Mario, to lighting mistakes in Luigi's Mansion, and of course, the truly messed up world of The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask - Nintendo has had some truly creepy moments in their games.

Spooky easter eggs in Mario have included moments like the Shiverburn Galaxy "Hell Valley Sky Trees" of Super Mario Galaxy 2. Or perhaps, a genuine ghost that appears in the ghost houses of Super Mario 3D Land.
