A range of applications are creating a wave of biometrics for banking and payments, which is buoying Fourthline, iDenfy, Socure, Bonifii and iProov, among many others, while American Express added a biometric authentication option for online payments. Pragmatic presented its vision for a new alternative to silicon chips for fingerprint sensors that can be used in payment cards and numerous other applications.
