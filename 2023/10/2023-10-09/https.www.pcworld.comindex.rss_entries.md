<p>Anyone who frequently installs and uninstalls new software, tests beta versions of programs, or tinkers with the registry in Windows, often reaches the point where they have to completely re-install their operating system. With the help of a <strong>virtual machine</strong>, you can save yourself the effort. But even if you want to use Linux as a second operating system as easily as possible, a virtual PC is just the thing for you.</p>
