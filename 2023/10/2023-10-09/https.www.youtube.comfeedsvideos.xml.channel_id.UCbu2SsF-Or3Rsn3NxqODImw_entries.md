Lords of the Fallen is a spiritual successor to the 2014 game of the same name and acts as a full reboot of the franchise. Here's everything you need to know about this upcoming action RPG. 

Lords of the Fallen is an action RPG developed by Hexworks and published by CI Games. In it you play as a Dark Crusader who is tasked with preventing the return of the dark god Adyr. To do so, you must overcome the horrors of not only the world of the living, but the land of the dead too. 

In this video, we'll take you through everything you need to know about Lords of the Fallen (2023), from the game's plot, gameplay mechanics, including shifting between two parallel worlds, release date, platforms, and editions of the game.
