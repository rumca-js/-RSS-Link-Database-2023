## Brazil's Serra da Capivara cave paintings are under threat
 - [https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/article/2023/11/04/brazil-s-serra-da-capivara-cave-paintings-are-under-threat_6226650_10.html](https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/article/2023/11/04/brazil-s-serra-da-capivara-cave-paintings-are-under-threat_6226650_10.html)
 - RSS feed: https://www.lemonde.fr/en/science/rss_full.xml
 - date published: 2023-11-04T10:00:10+00:00

This natural park in the Nordeste state of Piaui is a 130,000-hectare environmental reserve and open-air art gallery. But the UNESCO World Heritage site is suffering from the effects of mass tourism, climate change and financial difficulties.
