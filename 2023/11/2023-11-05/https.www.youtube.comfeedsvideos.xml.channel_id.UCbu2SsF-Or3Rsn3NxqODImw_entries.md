The Dragon of Dojima is back in Like A Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name. Here’s everything you need to know about the upcoming game. 

Like A Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name is the next installment in the long running Like A Dragon series, formerly known as Yakuza. Gaiden continues the story of original series protagonist Kazuma Kiryu after he faked his own death to protect his family, and takes place between Yakuza 6: The Song Of Life and Yakuza: Like A Dragon. 

In this video, we go over everything you need to know from gameplay, its return to brawler combat, Kiryu's return, and the role it plays leading into the next mainline game, Like A Dragon: Infinite Wealth. 

Like A Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name is set to launch on November 9th for PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X and S. It will only be available digitally in the West.
