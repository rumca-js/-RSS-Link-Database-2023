Untold Legends reimagines the story of the Trojan war, except something goes horribly wrong and the protagonist is seemingly thrown several years into the future, surrounded by undead enemies and even demons. Initially tasked by a mysterious voice to look for a smith, the protagonist has to figure out just what’s going on in the game's world.

Unfortunately, despite its clever setup and interesting ideas in gameplay, execution falls quite short in the loot-based action RPG featuring Souls-styled combat. The fights never really feel satisfying, and a complete waste of time at worst. Legends Untold has the bones of a good game, but its ideas never really come together as well as they should have.
