## Fighting 3 Lynels At Once, Damage-less | Zelda Tears of the Kingdom Gameplay

Its possible in Tears of the Kingdom without mods to take on three Lynels at once. By setting travel medallions behind two Lynels in the Floating Colosseum and ancient arrowing them, we can set the other 3 to release all at once. We do so without taking any damage.
