Games based around a devastating apocalypse have proven to be one of the most popular genres in the medium for some time now, and plenty of developers are working on varied titles that explore this theme in new and interesting ways. Post-apocalyptic games today aren’t just limited to RPGs, but also encompass shooters, horror games, city builders, and much more. 

From highly anticipated releases like State of Decay 3 and Death Stranding 2 to ambitious projects like PIONER and Road to Vostok, there’s no shortage of post-apocalyptic games to look forward to in the coming months and years.
