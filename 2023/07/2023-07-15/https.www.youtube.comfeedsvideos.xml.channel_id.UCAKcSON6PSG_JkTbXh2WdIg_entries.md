American folk band Joseph from Portland, Oregon visited The Current studios in June of 2023 during their stop in the Twin Cities, and played a sold out show at the Fitzgerald Theater. The three sisters released their latest album The Sun in April of 2023. 

Natalie, Allison and Meegan performed three songs from their most recent album and chatted with host Mac Wilson.
