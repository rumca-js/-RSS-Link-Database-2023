 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VRMXLNzWfFI](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VRMXLNzWfFI)
 - RSS feed: https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=UCbu2SsF-Or3Rsn3NxqODImw
 - date published: 2023-06-08 21:00:14+00:00

Lies of P is an upcoming 3rd person action game with a distinct "souls-like" style.

The game is inspired by Carlo Collodi's The Adventures of Pinocchio, involving a puppet takeover where puppets have gone mad and begun attacking everyone. While puppets are hated, Pinocchio seems distinct, and is able to break general puppet rules. Not being able to lie is a core Tennant of a puppet, and yet, one that Pinocchio can clearly break.

Lies of P releases August 2023 to PS5, PS4, Xbox Series X/S, Xbox One, and Steam
