This feature highlights video game bosses which, whilst initially presenting a threat to the player, go on to become allies, companions, or offerors of assistance to some degree once defeated. This assistance may take the form of a reward or powerup – something which aids the player onward as they continue the game’s main questline. 

Other times, the defeated boss becomes a true friend to the player character, subverting the usual role a boss character takes in a video game’s story. Ordinarily, their function is as a test to player skill with defeated bosses usually killed for good.
